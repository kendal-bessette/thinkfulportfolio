My Story

Hi there! I am a software developer and filmmaker living in Brooklyn, New York. Over the past decade I have worked in tech and operations for organizations including:

  • Alamo Drafthouse Cinema
  • SXSW
  • Sundance Film Festival
  • Telluride FIlm Festival

Currently, I am the Technical Manager for the London-based film distributor, Together Films, and the Co-Founder of the Brooklyn-based production company, Hot Lamp Films.



I have been interested in expanding my technical knowledge for years and have finally decided to take the plunge and become a full-fledged developer! After graduating from Thinkful I hope to use my new skills to:

  1. Work with documentary filmmakers to build websites that can help amplify the message of their social impact campaigns.
  2. Volunteer in my community to help local organizations build websites and libraries of resources for the community.
  3. Collaborate with other developers in the film industry to increase the accessibility of virtual screenings and events.
  4. Mentor other young women working in tech and film that are looking to make the leap to software development.
